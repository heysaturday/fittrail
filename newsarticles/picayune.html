Picayune & Poplarville School Districts, Pearl River County, Mississippi

As part of a campaign to promote exercise and improved health for students in Pearl River County, the Picayune and the Poplarville School Districts combined to purchase nine Fit-Trail systems over a one-year span in 2005 and 2006. Their continued repeat purchases were an indication of the receptivity the Fit-Trail systems enjoyed, and by consolidating their volume purchases the Districts were able to save on motor freight by hiring a contract hauler for delivery.
