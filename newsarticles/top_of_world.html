On Top of the World Communities

At the 12,000-acre active adult retirement community outside Ocala FL, thousands of residents have access to golf courses, swimming pools, tennis courts and recreation centers. As a way to offer yet another type of recreational amenity to the extensive trail system already in place, management decided to add (3) 10-station Fit-Trail systems to supplement the trails. One benefit of a Fit-Trail is that once it is in place, there are no operating costs, staff supervision, or regular maintenance required, making it an attractive recreational amenity to provide residents.
