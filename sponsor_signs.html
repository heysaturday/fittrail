Custom Sponsor Signs

For those who want to recognize donors or sponsors who provided funding or support for their Fit-Traill, we offer 3 options:

  Jumbo Donor Signs - Uses the same wood frame and sign panel size (24" x 48") as our Introductory Signs. These jumbo donor signs offer ample room to thank/recognize donors, and tell their story. Using client provided logos, text, or artwork, we will help you craft suitable recognition for your sponsor(s).
   
  Regular Donor Signs - Similar to the above, this version uses a wood frame and sign panel size (16" x 24") as on the Station Signs of our 10 and 20 station systems. Like the jumbo donor signs we will help you craft custom sponsor recognition, with 1/3 the message area as our jumbo size.
   
  Donor Identity Strips - Limited to simply a name or logo, these 2-1/2" x 7" strips are provided without a wood frame, and can be easily affixed at installation time to the wooden cross member of each station sign.

 

Note that all donor signs must be ordered and shipped with your Fit-Trail system. Prior to manufacture, you will receive a proof of your custom donor sign, which must be approved and returned to us as directed.

Pricing for custom donor signs is as follows, and includes "normal" artwork prep charges. Your account representative can advise you if additional charges will apply once we have received your artwork and intended message.

  Jumbo Donor Sign $995
     
  Regular Donor Sign $550
     
  Donor Identity Strip $50 (Quantity of 1)
    $200 (10 Identical)
    $350 (20 Identical)
