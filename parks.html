Parks & Recreation

In hundreds of parks, Fit-Trail systems have become a regular amenity along with tennis
courts, pools and sports fields. Organizations of all types join with park departments in
co-sponsoring and dedicating Fit-Trail systems as goodwill community projects.

Among the thousands of Fit-Trail users worldwide over the past 30+ years can be found
the following:

  • Boston Parks - Boston MA
  • County of Fort Walton Beach - Fort Walton Beach FL
  • Shenandoah County Parks - Woodstock VA
  • Mayaguez Park - Mayaguez PR
  • City of McAllen - McAllen TX
  • Harvey Cedars Park - Harvey Cedars NJ
  • Hayo-Went-Ha YMCA - Central Lake MI
  • Boca Terra Park - Boca Raton FL
  • Town of Henrietta - Henrietta NY
  • Jeep Rogers Family YMCA - Columbia SC
  • City of Arvada - Arvada CO
  • Cleveland Metroparks - Cleveland OH
  • Rails to Trails of the Withlacoochee - Inverness FL
  • City of Santa Fe - Santa Fe TX
